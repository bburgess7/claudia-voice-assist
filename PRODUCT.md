# PRODUCT.md — Claudia

## Register
Product (design serves the tool). A control surface for a local voice assistant.

## What / who / why
Claudia is a local, Jarvis-style conversational voice layer for macOS that speaks a *summary* of
what's happening (e.g. narrates Claude Code's results, not its raw code). This surface is the
control panel: tune the voice, speed, verbosity; see what she's saying; talk to her. It doubles as
the mobile/remote client over Tailscale. One user (Ben), hands-free, often not looking at the screen.

## Brand personality
Calm, capable, present. A smoldering intelligence in a dark workshop — not a cold robot. Confident
restraint over flashy sci-fi.

## Anti-references (what to NOT look like)
- **Cyan/ice-blue "Jarvis" HUDs.** The saturated cliché. We go warm ember/oxblood instead.
- Cheesy sci-fi (Orbitron, glowing hexagons, fake "scanning" chrome).
- Generic dark SaaS dashboard (gray cards on gray, one teal accent).
- Glassmorphism-everywhere.

## Design principles
- The voice is the product; the UI is quiet around one living focal point (the orb).
- Two states matter: **idle** (calm, breathing) and **speaking** (reactive, alive). Everything
  else is controls that get out of the way.
- Legible at a glance from across the room AND usable one-handed on a phone.
